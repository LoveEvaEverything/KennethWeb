Trade Minister in hot water after shouting 'You're not in Mexico now'

Trade Minister Todd McClay had to apologise to Parliament after shouting “you’re not in Mexico now” at Green MP Ricardo Menendez March.

His outburst came during a raucous question time on Tuesday, where Health Minister Shane Reti was grilled about the state of Te Whatu Ora and NZ First’s Shane Jones talked up his love of mining.

Apology over Mexico comment

Menendez March raised a point of order, saying McClay’s heckling during question time had been offensive.
